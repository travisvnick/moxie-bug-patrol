Moxie Technician — 3 Style Options

Same Moxie branding, different character proportions. Pick your favorite!

Option A

Chibi Compact

Big head, small body · Cute & expressive · Crossy Road vibes
Vibe: Adorable, toylike. Head is ~60% of the character. Big expressive eyes that kids connect with immediately. Works great at small game sizes because the face is so readable.
Best for: Younger kids (6-9), maximum cuteness factor.
Option B

Athletic Teen

Taller, action-ready · Confident stance · Sneaky Sasquatch proportions
Vibe: Capable, energetic, ready for action. More realistic proportions but still cartoony. Name badge adds a personal touch. Utility belt with pouches. This is the Sneaky Sasquatch proportion range.
Best for: Broader age range (7-12), feels like a real character you'd play as.
Option C

Rounded Friendly

Soft shapes, warm feel · Untitled Goose Game style · Maximum approachability
Vibe: Soft, huggable, warm. All circles and ellipses — no sharp edges anywhere. Dot eyes with rosy cheeks give an innocent, friendly feel. Think Untitled Goose Game or Fall Guys.
Best for: Maximum warmth and approachability. The character you want to protect.
All three use Moxie brand colors: Blue #0C77D8 · Navy #123250 · Light blue uniform shirt · Black Moxie cap with diamond logo
